About The Site

The Blue Hydrangea is a platform for creating your own blog or journal! It's based upon a simple content management system (CMS) which includes some of the following features:

  • administrators can create content using Markdown or regular HTML,
  • a convenient interface for managing accounts and content,
  • a permissions system that can apply to both individual accounts and groups.

How can I get an account here?

After you have read over the privacy policy and terms of service, please contact me and include the following information in your e-mail:

  • your desired username (this should not exceed 150 characters and be alphanumeric, e.g. Guest1),
  • your full name,
  • your e-mail address.

Your full name and e-mail address will only be visible to administrators. This information will not be shared without your permission. If your account has been approved, you will be contacted with a temporary password which you can then reset to any value of your choosing!
