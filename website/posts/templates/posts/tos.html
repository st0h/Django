Privacy Policy

The Blue Hydrangea has been designed to collect a minimum amount of data in order to operate. Our web server software logs the following information when you visit:

  • the IP address from which the request originated,
  • the URL requested,
  • the web browser used to visit the site (as well as its version),
  • the link which referred you to the site.

In addition, the following storage areas provided by your browser may be used to temporarily store information related to your session:

  • cookies,
  • local storage,
  • session storage.

None of these bits of information are used to track your activity across any other website. They are strictly used to store information related to your account and session, e.g. whether or not you are an authenticated user.

Terms of Service

The Blue Hydrangea is dedicated to providing a professional, safe environment for online discussions. The following guidelines must be followed if you intend to post or comment on the site:

  • do not use abusive or hateful language,
  • do not post content which is considered illegal (e.g. links to piracy sites),
  • do not attempt to interfere with site operation, impersonate staff members or access portions of the site that are restricted to moderators or staff members,
  • do not use the site for advertising of any kind (exceptions may be made for charitable organizations or fundraisers).

Any content or user that fails to adhere to these guidelines will be immediately and permanently removed!
